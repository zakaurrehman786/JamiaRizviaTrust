Our Legacy and Leadership

Providing Authentic Islamic and Quranic Education to the Global Community Since 1984

ESTABLISHED 1984

Bridging Sacred Tradition with Modern Education

Jamia Rizvia Trust was founded in 1984 with the core vision of spreading authentic Islamic knowledge, character development, and academic excellence. For over four decades, our parent organization has served as a beacon of Islamic learning, nurturing scholars and guiding students with sincerity and academic integrity.

With the launch of Jamia Rizvia Online Quran Academy, we extend this legacy to the digital space, offering international Muslim parents in the UK, USA, and Europe a trustworthy platform for their children's spiritual and Quranic development.

Our unique approach combines classical Islamic curriculum standards with modern, professional online teaching methodologies. Through interactive 1-to-1 live sessions, we ensure every student receives personalized attention in a supportive, encouraging, and safe online learning environment.

FOUNDER

Late Dr Mufti Ghulam Sarwer Qadri (RA)

1939 - 2010

Dr. Mufti Ghulam Sarwer Qadri (RA) was a visionary Islamic scholar, jurist, and educator who dedicated his life to teaching and community service. He established Jamia Rizvia Trust in 1984, aiming to create institutions where authentic religious sciences could be taught alongside modern subjects.

His legacy lives on through the thousands of graduates who serve communities worldwide, and the academy continues to uphold the standards of dedication, high ethics, and depth of knowledge he pioneered.
